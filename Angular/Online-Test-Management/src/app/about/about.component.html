Team Gauss

This Online Test Management System is a result of our joint effort to complete the project.
This Project was assigned to us in the Sprint-2 of Capgemini training, to evaluate our understanding of concepts learned during the training period and teamwork.
We thank Capgemini to provide this opportunity to us, and our trainers and mentor for their support.
Our team is named after Johann Carl Friedrich Gauss, a German mathematician and physicist who made significant contributions to many fields in mathematics and science.
This project is also a little contribution of us to help people by providing them a platform to test their knowledge and learning.
